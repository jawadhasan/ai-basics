## What is an AI Agent

An automated system that can take actions on your behalf. It is powered by GenAI and an LLM that understands user intent within a specific context.

When it comes to working with an AI agents, is to delegate Tasks, not Jobs to AI.

Agents have three core capabilities.

1. Conext
2. Reasoning
3. Acting

## Options for Creating Agents

- Managed: Batteries included, delivered to you as a simple service.
 - Jasper
 - Claude Skills
 - ChatGPT

- Low or No-code: Some assembly required, built with visual tools.
 - Zapier Agents
 - Flowise Agentflow
 - Microsoft Copilot Studio

- From Scratch: Totally custom, built with code, APIs, and SDKs.
 - MS Agent Framework
 - MastraAI
 - CrewAI

## Principles for Creating Effective Agents

- Delegate tasks, not jobs
- Contxt is King
- Least Privillege
- Human in the loop (HIL)
- Audit your agents
- Don't outsource your thinking
