# License Categories

## Permissive Licenses

- Do almost anything you want.
- Allow research + commercial use.
- Modify, integrate, build a business.
- Examples: Falcon 40 - Apache 2.0

## Custom Community Licenses

- More restrictive.
- Commercial use allowed with conditions.
- Requirements and limitatoins apply.
- Examples: Liama 4 - custom community license.
